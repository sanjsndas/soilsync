Privacy Policy

At SoilSync, your privacy and data security is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read it carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide to us such as your name, contact details, email address, and any other information you provide when filling forms or communicating with us. We also collect information automatically such as IP addresses, browser types, device information, and browsing behavior through cookies and analytics tools to ensure the best experience and service optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, respond to your inquiries, send service-related communications, and enhance your experience on our website. We may also use aggregate data for research, promotional efforts, or developing new features. We take care to only use your data in ways you have authorized or are legally permitted.

3. Data Sharing Practices

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with trusted service providers, partners, or regulatory authorities when necessary for business operations, legal compliance, or protection of rights. All partners are contractually obliged to maintain confidentiality and comply with applicable data protection regulations.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We employ cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information that helps provide personalized content, analyze site usage, and improve functionality. Cookies may also support marketing efforts and user preferences. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings; however, disabling certain cookies may impact website functionality.

5. User Account Information

If you create an account on SoilSync, we collect and store additional data such as username, password, and profile details. This information is used to authenticate your identity, provide personalized services, and communicate order status or updates. We encourage strong password protection practices and do not share account credentials with third parties.

6. Data Security Measures

SoilSync implements industry-standard security measures including encryption, multi-factor authentication, firewalls, and regular security audits to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to maintain security, please be aware no system is completely foolproof.

7. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. Retention periods may vary based on business needs or legal obligations related to record keeping.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, or object to certain uses. You also may have the right to data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond timely and in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Children’s Privacy

SoilSync’s services and website are not intended for individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians concerned about their children's data can contact us to inquire or request data removal.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. or other countries where SoilSync or its service providers operate. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws to safeguard your personal data rights.

11. Use of Analytics Services

SoilSync uses analytics services such as Google Analytics to gather information about website usage and user behavior. This data helps us optimize our content and services. These tools collect anonymous information and do not identify individual users.

12. Email Communication

We send transactional emails regarding your account, orders, or services you use. Occasionally, newsletters or marketing communications may be sent; you may opt out anytime. We do not share your email address with third-party marketers without explicit consent.

13. Third-Party Services

Some features on SoilSync rely on trusted third-party services, which may collect or process data as described in their policies. We encourage you to review their privacy statements to understand how they use your information.

14. Data Accuracy

It is your responsibility to provide accurate and current information. If your data changes, please update your account or contact us directly to ensure we maintain accurate records and can provide the best service possible.

15. Privacy Policy Changes

SoilSync reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in laws, practices, or technology. Material changes will be posted on our website with a clear effective date. We encourage users to periodically review this page.

16. Secure Payment Information

During payment processes, financial information is handled securely via encrypted channels by authorized third-party payment processors. SoilSync does not store payment card details on its servers to protect your financial privacy.

17. Social Media Integration

Our website may include social media buttons or plugins. These tools may collect your IP address and browsing data when you interact with them. This data collection is subject to the policies of the respective social media platforms.

18. Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach impacting your personal information, SoilSync will promptly notify affected users and regulatory authorities in compliance with data protection laws and follow a comprehensive security incident response plan.

19. User Consent

By using SoilSync's website or services, you consent to this Privacy Policy and the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

23. Automated Decision Making

25. Security Auditing
