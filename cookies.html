Cookies Policy

Welcome to the SoilSync Cookies Policy. This policy explains how SoilSync uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your experience, understand our audience, and improve services. We respect your privacy and provide transparent information regarding all data collection through cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to remember information about your visit. They help us personalize your experience, keep you logged in, and collect analytical data that allow us to monitor and improve our website performance. Cookies can be session-based or persistent, depending on their purpose.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use essential cookies necessary for site functionality, performance cookies to monitor site usage, and advertising cookies for personalized marketing. Essential cookies cannot be declined without impacting core site features, whereas performance and marketing cookies require your consent.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies collect anonymous data to analyze traffic patterns and user interactions. This data helps SoilSync optimize content placement, improve navigation, and tailor promotional offers. Cookies also maintain your preferences ensuring consistent experiences across sessions.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We integrate third-party services like analytics and social media platforms which may place their own cookies on your device. These third parties manage their cookies independently and have their privacy policies explaining their use of your data.

5. Cookie Consent

Upon your first visit, you will be presented with a cookie consent dialog allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences anytime using the banner or your browser settings. Accepting cookies lets us provide you with the best possible experience.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings through your web browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies altogether; however, some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Essential Cookies

These cookies are crucial for basic website operations, such as security, accessibility, and remembering your cookie preferences. They do not track your browsing activity or collect personal data beyond what is necessary.

8. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect aggregated anonymous information about how visitors use the website, including the pages they visit and any errors encountered. We use this data to improve site speed, content, and overall usability to better serve you.

9. Advertising Cookies

These cookies allow us and our partners to display relevant advertisements based on your interests and previous interactions with SoilSync. This makes advertising more relevant without exposing personally identifiable information.

10. Cookie Duration

Cookie duration varies: session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain for specified periods (weeks to years) to remember your preferences and interactions for subsequent visits.

11. Cookies and Security

Cookies do not contain viruses or malware, and they cannot be used to access your computer or personal files. They are purely for storing information that improves your website experience and functionality.

12. How to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clicking the cookie banner’s settings or clearing cookies in your browser. Once withdrawn, SoilSync will stop placing non-essential cookies on your device.

13. Cookies and Your Privacy

While cookies collect information about your website interactions, SoilSync handles this data with the utmost respect for your privacy, following data protection regulations and internal policies to keep information secure.

14. Analytics Cookies Detail

We implement analytics cookies such as Google Analytics, which gather data like time spent on pages, traffic sources, and device usage. This data is anonymized to prevent identification of individual users.

15. Social Media Widgets and Cookies

Embedded social media buttons may set cookies that track your interaction with our site and their platforms. These cookies support sharing features but are managed by the third parties providing them.

16. Cookie Policy Updates

This Cookies Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or SoilSync's operational practices. Material changes will be communicated. We advise reviewing this policy periodically.

17. Cookies on Mobile Devices

Cookies operate similarly on mobile devices, storing preferences and usage data. Mobile browsers provide controls similar to desktop, allowing you to manage cookie settings effectively.

18. Disabling Cookies Impact

Disabling cookies may impact your ability to use certain interactive or personalized features on the SoilSync website, including session management and customized content.

19. Cookies and Third-Party Advertising Networks

Third-party advertising cookies may collect your browsing information across sites to tailor ads. These networks operate independently; refer to their privacy policies for details.

20. User Rights Concerning Cookies

You have the right to choose whether to allow cookies and to understand how they are used. SoilSync provides transparency and control tools to assist you in managing your preferences effectively.

21. Cookie Consent Duration

Your cookie consent is remembered by SoilSync for one year. After which, you will be asked to review your preferences again to keep them up to date.

22. How to Access Browser Cookie Settings

Each browser has manuals or help sections explaining how to manage cookies. This typically includes viewing, blocking, or deleting cookies and managing permissions on a per-site basis.

23. Cookie Categories Overview

Categories include necessary, preference, statistics, marketing, and social media cookies. Each has specific functions explained in this policy to help you make informed decisions.

24. Cookie Identification

Every cookie has a unique identifier to track its purpose and expiry. SoilSync documents each cookie used on our site and their roles transparently.

25. Third-Party Cookie Providers

Providers include Google, Facebook, and other analytics or advertising partners. SoilSync selects partners carefully to ensure privacy compliance and data security.

26. Cookies on SoilSync Services

Besides the website, SoilSync services may use cookies to personalize software interfaces and remotely monitor devices installed on your farm, with your consent.

27. Importance of Cookies for Users

Cookies enhance your website experience by remembering login status, preferences, and delivering content optimized to your needs and past behavior.

28. Analytics Data Use

Analyzed data helps improve SoilSync’s usability, fix errors, and develop new features that meet your needs more effectively.

29. Cookies and Legal Compliance

SoilSync complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other data protection laws, ensuring lawful use of cookies and safeguarding your rights.

30. Children and Cookies

SoilSync sites are not intended for children under 13. Cookies are not used to collect data knowingly from minors, and we actively work to comply with child protection laws.

31. Withdrawal of Consent Process

Withdrawal can be done through cookie banner controls or browser settings at any time, and SoilSync will act promptly to comply.

32. Cookies and User Tracking

Cookies enable anonymous tracking to understand general user trends but are not used to identify individual users without consent.

33. Retention of Cookie Data

Cookie data is retained only for the purpose and period necessary, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

34. Cookie Storage

Cookies are stored locally on your device in encrypted format to prevent unauthorized access or tampering.

35. Cookies and Personalization

Personalized content using cookies includes language preferences, region selection, and tailored recommendations.

36. Third-Party Analytics Cookies

Third-party analytics providers use cookies that are governed by their own privacy policies; SoilSync does not control their use.

37. Disabling Cookies and Limitations

Disabling cookies may reduce site functionality, including login persistence and some interactive features; users should weigh convenience with privacy preferences.

38. Cookies and Advertising

SoilSync uses cookies to provide non-intrusive advertising aligned with your interests without collecting sensitive personal information.

39. Cookie Policy Changes

This policy may be updated periodically with changes posted on this page. Continued use after changes indicates your acceptance.
